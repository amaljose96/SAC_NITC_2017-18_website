'It always seems impossible until it is done.'
It is said that 'dreams and dedication are a powerful combination'. It is with this motto in mind that the Student affairs council (SAC) of NIT Calicut moves ahead. The students of the institute constitute its supreme power, the General Students Body (GSB). The Director of the institute is the patron of the GSB and the SAC along with the executive council (EC) function as the councils responsible for addressing the demands and aspirations of the GSB. As the SAC constitution states, the SAC 'shall function as the central policy making body'. These policies should be based on the requirements of the GSB. The EC is the body that is accountable to the SAC to execute and implement the policies of the SAC. In short, these bodies remain responsible to the well being of the entire student community and it is the constant objective of these to ensure the same.
